Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love every AuraPop product. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help.

Return Window

Returns and exchanges are accepted within 14 days of delivery.

Eligibility

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Opened cosmetics cannot be returned for hygiene reasons
  • Damaged or incorrect items are always eligible

How to Start a Return

Email us at support@toriedition.com with your order number.

Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method once the return is approved.
